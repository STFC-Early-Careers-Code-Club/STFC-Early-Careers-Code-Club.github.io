Ever wondered what a programming language made of emojis would look like? What about a programming language that uses only whitespace characters? In this short, fun talk, we'll be looking at esoteric programming languages, or 'esolangs' for short. Along the way, we'll consider the requirements of a programming language, what we in turn require from them, and finally explore some notable examples.
