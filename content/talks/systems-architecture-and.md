title: Systems Architecture and Agentic AI
speaker: Willow Sparks
pronouns: she/her
date: 2026-07-08
---
This talk will be about the underlying concepts and ideas for building large-scale systems in the context of agentic AI! I'll cover a few topics, such as:
- The actor model
- Tool calling, sandboxes and "code mode"
- Context Engineering and Harness Engineering
- Kubernetes and Orchestration
- Macro-agents, micro-agents and recursive language models (RLM)

By the end I hope to give a bit more insight into how agents work, and also draw some useful parallels to various other ideas in computing.
